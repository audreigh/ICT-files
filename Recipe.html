Sinigang na Bangus is a type of sour clear broth fish soup. This recipe that we have here is basic, but it is my favorite among all the versions that I have tried. My family likes this too because it is simple and delicious. You can really enjoy the fresh taste of the fish complemented by the warm sour broth. Sinigang na bangus is best served with warm rice. I also like to eat it using fish sauce as a condiment. Simply pour some fish sauce in a saucer and add a few pieces of chili pepper.

Sinigang na Bangus


Sinigang na bangus is a Filipino fish recipe, bangus or milkfish is stewed in a flavorful, sour broth with lots of vegetables for more added nutrients.

Course type:   Main Course.
Cuisine           Filipino
Keyword       sinigang, sinigang na bangus, fish
Prep Time       5 mins
Cook Time       Approximately 20 mins
Total Time       25 mins
Servings         3 people
Calories         1215kcal
Author         Audreigh Cyan Santos


Ingredients


  • 1 pc sibuyas, quarted
  • 1 pc kamatis sliced
  • 4 cups tubig
  • 1 pc bangus,hiniwa
  • 1 pc siling haba
  • 1/2 pc medium labanos, sliced
  • 1 pc talong, slice
  • 1 (20g) pakete sinigang mix orignal
  • 1/2 bundle kangkong, cut into serving pieces
  • 6 to 8 pieces okra


Instructions


  1. Combine onion, tomato and water in a pot. Boil for 5 minutes
  2. Add the bangus, siling haba, radish, eggplant, okra and rice mix original.
